How to contribute?

Customers can provide service feedback in (Branch, Mobile Banking, and Internet Banking)








1. Branch

When you visit one of our branches and complete your banking service, you can easily share your feedback using our dedicated tablets available within the branch.




2. Internet Banking

Using our Internet Banking platform, you can provide feedback after each service interaction. After completing a service or transaction on Internet Banking, a feedback popup will appear on your screen.




3. Mobile Banking

Using our Mobile Banking app, we encourage you to share your feedback. After completing a service or transaction in our Mobile Banking app, a feedback popup will appear. You can rate your satisfaction and provide additional comments if desired.
